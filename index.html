// About me

26 years.
One relentless craft.

I'm an independent brand and design specialist based in Singapore — an art director and designer with over 26 years of experience across branding, packaging, communications design, and 3D visualisation.

I've worked on notable brands including Apple, L'Oréal, Unilever, Dow Chemicals, American Express, Tiger Beer, Singtel, and Red Bull — through top agencies like Hogarth Worldwide, Ogilvy & Mather, DDB, Saatchi & Saatchi, and McCann-Erickson, as well as my own agency Blacksheep Communications, which I co-founded as Managing Director and Creative Director.
